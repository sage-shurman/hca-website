Our Mission

Beyond the Classroom

We complement formal coursework by making possible the kinds of ambitious, experimental, and collaborative projects that often exceed its scope.

HCA is a space where composers take risks, work across mediums, and develop original projects in conversation with performers, visual artists, technologists, writers, dancers, and directors.

With support from the Harvard Music Department and faculty advisor Professor Yvette Jackson

01 Fostering community among composers across styles and backgrounds
02 Creating opportunities for inter-genre collaboration
03 Building interdisciplinary partnerships with artists and technologists
04 Offering pre-professional artistic experiences and mentorship
05 Expanding access to performance and recording opportunities

Interdisciplinary · String Quartet · Electronics · AV

Paracosms

A paracosm is a "private- but- shared imagined world sufficiently rich in detail that people become engaged in the stories and can return to them again and again." Through this performance, HCA and Conflux create real-life paracosms through music, visual art, and technology.

7 original string quartets by Harvard undergraduates, some incorporating electronics, performed in Holden Chapel with flexible seating so the audience can roam freely through the projections.

Collaborators: Conflux Art-Tech Collective · Funded by the Office of the Arts at Harvard

How it works

String quartet + electronics — pitch, timbre, volume & beat
